## AI Assistant: Msaidizi

The local MVP version of Msaidizi answers only from approved service guide content bundled in the app. The future Supabase version should answer only from published guide content in the database. It must not invent fees, claim official authority, or guarantee approval.

Fallback phrase:

> Sina uhakika. Tafadhali hakiki kupitia tovuti rasmi au ofisi husika.

## Compliance Guardrails

- Always show independent guide disclaimer
- Clearly label official portal links as external
- Avoid exact fee claims unless verified
- Add "confirm with official source" near cost and process fields
- Use "general guidance, not legal/tax advice" for legal and tax content
