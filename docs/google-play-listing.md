## Full Description

HudumaGuide TZ helps people in Tanzania prepare for public services, business setup tasks, documents, and important deadlines. Use the app to find step-by-step preparation guides, save checklists, track required documents, create reminders, and organize your next action before visiting an office or starting an application.

Important disclaimer: HudumaGuide TZ is an independent guide. It does not represent any government entity and is not a government app. It is not affiliated with, authorized by, or endorsed by any government agency. The app does not submit applications, collect official fees, issue documents, guarantee approvals, or replace official government instructions. Always confirm final requirements, fees, deadlines, forms, payment instructions, and procedures through official government or institution channels before payment or submission.

Official government sources referenced by HudumaGuide TZ:

- NIDA Online Services for NIDA/NIN guidance and replacement support: https://services.nida.go.tz/
- Tanzania Revenue Authority Taxpayer Portal for TIN registration, taxpayer services, and tax deadline checks: https://taxpayerportal.tra.go.tz/
- Tanzania Immigration e-Services Passport Portal for passport applications: https://eservices.immigration.go.tz/online/web/passport
- eRITA Portal for birth certificate and civil registration services: https://erita.rita.go.tz/
- BRELA Online Registration System for business name and company registration: https://ors.brela.go.tz/
- Tanzania National Business Portal for business licence guidance: https://business.go.tz/obtain-a-business-license

Key features:

- Service preparation guides for common Tanzania civic and business tasks
- Swahili and English support
- Saved checklists for important steps
- Reminder tools for deadlines and renewals
- Document vault for metadata, expiry dates, and renewal planning
- Business roadmap tools for early-stage planning
- Msaidizi assistant for guide-based questions
- Official source URL references inside guide details and assistant citations
- Optional account sync with Supabase
- Local-only use for many core features

HudumaGuide TZ is designed to help you prepare and stay organized. Use the official source links above and the official links shown inside each guide for final decisions, payments, submissions, and legal or tax obligations.
